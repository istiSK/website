Welcome to my website!

I am a Hermann Weyl instructor at ETH Zürich. Previously, I was a postdoc at the Princeton Gravity Initiative. My advisor was Mihalis Dafermos. I was a PhD student at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics at University of Cambridge under the supervision of Claude Warnick.

My research interest is in the area of partial differential equations of hyperbolic type that are related to physical systems describing wave propagation, with main applications in general relativity.
